OFFICE & CUBICLE NUMBERING SYSTEM at UG

When asking someone to stop by your office or cube please provide your cube/office number not your mailstop. The cubicles and offices are set up to a specific numbering pattern while the mailstops are just numbers.

Office Numbering System: Odd office numbers are located on the outside of the building looking away from Merck. Even numbered offices are on the inside of the building along the courtyard or atrium. Numbering starts on the North end of the building (garage end) and gets progressively higher heading south (towards Sumneytown Pk). Examples of a usual office number are UG-4CDN010 or UG-1A015

An office such as UG-3CDS018 broken down means the following; the 3CDS stands for 3rd floor on the CD building along the CD South wing, the 018 even number means it is along the atrium.

Cubicle Numbering System: Cubicle numbers also start on the north end of the building in the 200 section. Between each cube break the next cube set will be one number higher (e.g. 201, 202, 203...) The letters start at A on the East (Beaver St side) side of a wing and head West (Dickerson Road side). A typical cubicle number is UG-3B203A or UG-2D202H.

An cubicle such as UG-2A202Q broken down means the following; the 2A stands for 2nd floor of the AB building in the A Wing, the 202 means it is in the second section of cubicles and the Q stands for the exact cubicle location within that 2A202 section towards the west end.

Conference Room Numbering: UG conference rooms are listed in Outlook using the following format UG#letter(s)-#. The fist number stands for floor, followed by the hallway letter and ending with room number. For example UG-1A101 or UG-4CDS129.

Mailstop Numbering: Mailstops are listed as UG+floor+letter(s)-number. For example UG2CD-60 or UG3B-14.
